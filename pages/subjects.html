How Subject Mentorship Works

Every subject plan is built for clarity, confidence, and progress.

We diagnose learning gaps, match students with the right tutor, and track weekly outcomes so parents always know what is happening.

We start with a quick assessment to identify topics that need immediate support.

Match with an expert tutor

Track progress weekly

We monitor performance and adjust the plan to keep results consistent.

Keep parents informed

Parents receive updates on topics covered, improvements, and next steps each week.
